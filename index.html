Portfolio

After earning my undergraduate degree in computer science from Harvard, I moved to New York City to join Palantir as a Forward Deployed Engineer. My professional passion lies primarily in the growing intersection of technology, privacy, and government. Over the past few years, I have had the opportunity to work at the Federal Trade Commission, a data use protection startup, and with professors at the vanguard of privacy research. My study of a privacy vulnerability of dark net markets was covered by international media—read about it and my other work below!
